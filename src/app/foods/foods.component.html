Local Dishes

Special Delicacies Must Eat In Palawan.

Palawan may be famous for its natural wonders, but this island paradise in the Philippines also steps up its game when it comes to local dishes. If you wish to journey to this island destination, you might want to consider trying out these delectable treats. Here are the local delicacies to try when you visit Palawan.

Garlic Butter Shrimp

It is simply made of Shrimp, garlic, butter, and lemon soda. Don't take the lemon soda for granted because it is a major ingredient. It refers to soda pop such as Sprite, 7Up, Sierra Mist, and the likes. The sweet taste of this dish is attributed to this ingredient.

Sizzling Tuna Sisig

Sizzling Tuna Sisig is a tuna steaks sliced into small cubes are used instead of minced pork. This dish is served over a hot metal plate (also referred to as sizzling plate) and topped with crushed chicharon. It is served with chili pepper and lime or calamansi.

Special Buko Pandan

Buko Pandan is a Dessert made using young coconut, pandan leaves (or Screwpine leaves), and sago pearls. At first glance, this dessert dish can be mistaken for Buko Salad. Both desserts are almost similar visually.
